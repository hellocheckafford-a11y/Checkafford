Why BNPL feels cheaper than it is

Splitting $1,200 into four $300 payments feels painless. But you're still spending $1,200.

BNPL providers count on this psychological trick. Our calculator shows the real monthly impact.

When BNPL actually makes sense

BNPL is useful for necessities if the plan is interest-free and less than 5% of your income.

It's dangerous for discretionary spending. If you can't pay cash, BNPL delays the problem.

Common questions

Does BNPL affect my credit score?
It depends. Providers may report missed payments which damage your credit score.
Is 0% BNPL really free?
Only if you pay on time. Late fees and deferred interest traps can make it very expensive.
