Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CheckAfford.com collects, uses, and protects information when you use our free loan affordability calculators. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

CheckAfford.com is designed to protect your privacy. Our calculators work entirely in your browser — the financial numbers you enter (income, debts, loan amounts) are never sent to our servers and are not stored anywhere.

We may collect the following non-personal information automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous usage data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our tools so we can improve them
  • Monitor site performance and fix technical issues
  • Analyze which calculators are most useful to our visitors

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. Affiliate Links

Some links on CheckAfford.com are affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you click a link and complete an application or purchase — at no additional cost to you.

We only recommend financial products and services that we believe may be genuinely useful. Our affiliate relationships do not influence our calculator results or financial information.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our site (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies: Placed by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements
  • Functional cookies: To remember your language preference (EN/ES)

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites including lenders, financial services, and affiliate partners. These websites have their own privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit through our links.

7. Children's Privacy

CheckAfford.com is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

8. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal data (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
