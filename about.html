✦ About Us

Built for people who want honest answers.

CheckAfford is a free financial tool that helps everyday Americans figure out if they can really afford a loan — before signing anything.

Our Story

Why we built this

Most people find out they cannot afford a loan after they apply — when the rejection hits their credit score, or when the monthly payments start squeezing their budget.

We built CheckAfford because the question "Can I actually afford this?" deserves a clear, honest answer in seconds — not after a hard credit pull, not after talking to a salesperson.

Our calculators use the same debt-to-income formulas that lenders use. We show you the result in plain English — before you commit.
